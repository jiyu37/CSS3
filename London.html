London

London is the capital city of England. It is the most populous city in the United Kingdom, with a metropolitan area of over 13 million inhabitants.

The climate in Londom has a temperate oceanic climate. Summers are generally warm and sometimes hot. The average temperature of the month of July is 24 Celsius. Snow fall occurs time to time. London has a considerable urban heat island effect, making the centre of London at times 5 ℃ (9 ℉ ) warmer than the suburbs and outskirts
